Rajah was a Postdoctoral researcher with us from  2024 to the end of 2025. After a PhD from TUM, during which he studied frustrated spin systems, he worked on applying NQS to the same problems.

Studied what are the key ingredients to devise effective NQS when working with spin systems ["Design principles of deep translationally-symmetric neural quantum states for frustrated magnets"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.03466), and later applied it to [complex 3D geometries](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10008) relevant to experimental settings.

He left us at the end of 2025 to go back to London and embard into new adventures!
